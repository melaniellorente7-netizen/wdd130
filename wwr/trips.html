Featured Trips

Canal de los Dientes

Experience the most thrilling rapids in the region. Perfect for experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush. Navigate through challenging whitewater while enjoying stunning canyon views. This trip includes professional guides and all necessary safety equipment.

Big Mallard Adventure

A family-friendly rafting experience perfect for all skill levels. Float down gentle rapids while spotting wildlife and enjoying the natural beauty of the river valley. Great for beginners and families looking to bond over a shared adventure with expert guides.

Half Day Expedition

Discover hidden waterfalls and scenic overlooks on this moderately challenging trip. Navigate through moderate rapids while learning about local geology and ecology. Includes lunch and refreshments. Perfect for those seeking a balanced adventure experience.
